About

Get ready to experience the ultimate blend of culture, technology, and adrenaline at EssenCSE 2.0! Hosted by the prestigious School of CSE at REVA University, this fest promises an electrifying lineup of over 30 events, including captivating dance performances, soul-stirring singing competitions, mind-bending murder mysteries, pulse-pounding treasure hunts, and even an exhilarating real-life adaptation of the popular game "Among Us". Open to all students of REVA University, EssenCSE 2.0 is your ticket to a whirlwind of excitement, creativity, and unforgettable memories. Join us for a celebration like no other and unleash your talents in an atmosphere buzzing with energy and camaraderie!
